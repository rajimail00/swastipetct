Meet our Expert Doctor

Dr. Arun Sasikumar

Dr. Arun Sasikumar, a renowned Nuclear Medicine expert, currently serves as Managing Director and Chief Consultant at Swasti PET/CT Imaging and Nuclear Medicine in Trivandrum. With 12 years of clinical experience, he previously led the Department of Nuclear Medicine at St Gregorios Medical Mission Hospital and was a Consultant at KIMS hospital. His educational background includes an MD in Nuclear Medicine from PGIMER, Chandigarh, and he holds fellowships from European and Asian Boards of Nuclear Medicine. Dr. Sasikumar has 37 published articles, serves as an International Research Consultant in Kuwait, and is the Secretary of SNMI - SC since 2023.
