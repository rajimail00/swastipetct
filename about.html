Can I request a copy of my MRI online?

To obtain a copy of your MRI scan results, you can contact the medical facility where the scan was performed. Most facilities have a designated medical records department that can assist you in obtaining a copy. You may need to fill out a release form, and there could be a nominal fee for the copying service. Additionally, your healthcare provider may have access to the results and can provide you with a copy or discuss the findings with you.

What is a PET/CT scan, and how does it work?

A PET/CT scan combines positron emission tomography (PET) and computed tomography (CT) to provide detailed images of internal structures. PET detects metabolic activity, while CT offers anatomical details, resulting in comprehensive diagnostic information.

How long does a PET/CT scan take?

Are there any risks or side effects associated with PET/CT scans?

Risks are minimal, and side effects are rare. Some individuals may experience minor discomfort from injection sites or reactions to the radioactive tracer. Our experienced staff ensures your comfort and safety throughout the process.
